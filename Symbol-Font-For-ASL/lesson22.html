Lesson 22: Hand Locations 2

Hand location symbols combine a handshape symbol with other symbols to specify a location on the hand.

{``B}
back of the fingers
{B``}
between fingers / thumb
{..B}
pinky side of the hand
{B..}
thumb side of the hand

To indicate a specific finger, you may need to include a different hand shape. Put the main handshape outside the { }

{`1}5
tip of index finger,
5 handshape
{`v}5
tip of middle finger,
5 handshape
{`w}5
tip of ring finger,
5 handshape
{`i}5
tip of pinky,
5 handshape
