Facial movement

A sequence of mouths can be included in one symbol to show mouth movement, using a nose symbol to set each mouth apart from the next. A repeated nose indicates that the movement is repeated.

:%][%()
pah
:%][%()%*
pow
:%{[]}%()

:%]'[%%%()'
la-la-la
:%[]%%%()
sta-sta-sta
:%](%%%()
