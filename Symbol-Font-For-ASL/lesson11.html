Lesson 11: Hand Locations 1

Hand Location Symbols

Hand location symbols combine a handshape symbol with other symbols to specify a location on the hand.

{`B}
fingertips
{B`}
tip of the thumb
{.B}
back of the hand
{B.}
center of the palm
{B^}
above the other hand
{B_}
below the other hand
{B/}
in front of the other hand
{BZ}
behind the other hand
