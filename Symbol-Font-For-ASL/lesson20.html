Lesson 20: Palm Orientation 2

Orientation symbols combine a finger symbol and a thumb symbol to make a picture of the hand (from the signers perspective).

" = ' - % ~
finger symbols
. `
thumb symbols

With these symbols you can represent 24 possible hand orientations, although only 9 of them are commonly used. Six common orientations were explained in Lesson 7.
