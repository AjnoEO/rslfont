Head Movement Symbols

(__)
Head nod
(<>)
Head shake
(/)
Head forward
(Z)
Head back

Body Movement Symbols

{<}
Shift left
{>}
Shift right

Using Head/Body Movement Symbols

Head movement can be used to modify the meaning of a sign.

(/) (.)*L??
Who?
(Z) (..)**X
(Very) Recent
(__) s__
Yes
(<>) H__N
No

Body shifting can be used to make comparisons.

{|}*1 {`}*M
I have
{<} k??g 2
2 dogs
{>} (|)++f>> 3
(and) 3 cats
(__) {|}*1 {|}+D |<|1/
I like them (the dogs)
(<>) |>|1/
(Not) them (the cats).
