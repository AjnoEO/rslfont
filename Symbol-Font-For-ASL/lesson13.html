Lesson 13: Circular Movement

Circular Movement Symbols

@
Around in a circle
Circle first
@_
Around in a circle, then down
@<
Around in a circle, then left
@Z
Around in a circle, then back
Direction first
__@
Down, then circle around and down again
<<@
Left, then circle around and left again
//@
Forward, then circle around and forward again
ZZ@
Back, then circle around and back again
