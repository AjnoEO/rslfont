Using Facial Expression Symbols

Facial expression can be used to modify the meaning of a sign.

:%) {.}*O?c
(good) self-esteem
]:[%* BB<}{
small
({:}%() T(`|`)T^l}{
surprise
:[%[] (..)**X
(very) recent
]:%]'[ 33_^_}
awkward
(]:%{][} s."".s//)
(enjoying) driving

Facial expression can be used to indicate the grammatical structure of a sentence.

(:%| 1/ {|}+D (.)&1_ 1/
Do you like red?
}:%| t><> 1><>
Where is the bathroom?
(:%| {`}&w//@ dc
(__) 5.'>." {.}*P {|}*1
I have been to Washington, D.C.
